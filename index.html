About me

I'm a versatile ICT professional working as Large Clients Manager for SpazioDati in Trento, building and enhancing Atoka, a company information and B2B sales intelligence service. I work in synergy with developers and Cerved sales representatives in order to engage, design and coordinate the delivery of complex projects for the largest Italian corporations.

I'm also the President of Speck&Tech, a community I co-founded in Trento back in 2016, and former President of the EIT Digital Alumni, a pan-European network of tech professionals. I have a solid tech and UX background, having completed a double Master's Degree in HCI, studying both in Paris and Berlin, and a bachelor in Computer Science.

I spend a great amount of my free time running, biking and hiking around the beautiful mountains of Trentino and beyond. Always open to meet people and discuss possible collaborations and synergies: please send me a custom message, and let's connect!
