Hi, I'm Francesco!

I love UX and Interaction design.

About me

I am a young ICT professional currently working on my Master Thesis at Telekom Innovation Laboratories and completing my double degree at the EIT Digital Master School in Berlin (after spending my first year in Paris).

I am extremely interested in UX and the HCI field in general, with a particular focus on Innovation and startups. I am very passionate about sketching and prototyping, as well as presenting and pitching.

I am looking for positions as UX / UI Designer, however I am open to other opportunities.



Download my CV

skills

UX Design

I am particularly into User Experience and Interaction Design. I am familiar with techniques such as prototyping, wireframing, user-studies, Usability Evaluation and User-Centered Design.

I love sketching mockups and testing them with the users. I am skilled at understanding user-needs and turning them into different design solutions, creating surveys for collecting feedbacks and analysing the results.

Development

In the projects I worked at I was mostly in charge of the UX and UI design, focusing on the patterns for Android applications and helping the developers during the implementation phase.

I am becoming acquainted with Material Design guidelines and Java / Android programming languages; at the moment, moreover, I am focusing on Web Development. I am familiar with Eclipse, GitHub and Agile Development (Scrum).

Qualifications

I am such a precise person, a fast-learner and a hard-worker. I love trying early-releases and giving feedback to the developers. I am used to work in multilingual and multicultural teams.

I enjoy writing detailed reports, research papers and formal documents; I took academic writing courses and I can speak / write fluently English, Italian and Spanish, plus I have good written knowledge of German and basic French.
